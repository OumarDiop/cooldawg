How Electricity Providers Impact Climate Change

Energy sources have a big impact on the environment. Renewable energy is energy that comes from natural resources. Some renewable energy sources are: solar, wind, water, nuclear, biomass, and geothermal. Over 75% of the electricity worldwide is produced by burning of fossil fuels. If people started buying their energy from electric companies that used renewable energy sources, they would be helping the process of stopping climate change.
